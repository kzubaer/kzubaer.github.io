About Me

Passionate Chemistry Researcher

I am a dedicated researcher specializing in supramolecular chemistry, with a focus on understanding how molecules interact through weak forces to create complex, functional structures. My work bridges the gap between fundamental science and practical applications.

Currently pursuing my research at the University of South Carolina under the guidance of the Linda Shimizu Group, I am committed to advancing the field through innovative synthetic methodologies and cutting-edge analytical techniques.

Research Interests

🧬 Molecular Self-Assembly

Investigating the principles governing the spontaneous organization of molecules into ordered structures and their applications in creating functional materials for nanotechnology and biomedical applications.

🔗 Host-Guest Chemistry

Exploring molecular recognition and binding between host and guest molecules, with focus on selectivity and binding affinity optimization for drug delivery systems.

💊 Drug Delivery Systems

Developing supramolecular carriers for targeted drug delivery, utilizing non-covalent interactions for controlled release mechanisms and enhanced bioavailability.

🧪 Stimuli-Responsive Materials

Designing smart materials that respond to external stimuli such as pH, temperature, and light for applications in sensors, actuators, and adaptive materials.

Publications & Research

"Novel Supramolecular Architectures for Targeted Drug Delivery"
Journal of Supramolecular Chemistry, 2024 • In Preparation
Currently investigating novel supramolecular architectures for biomedical applications, with emphasis on stimuli-responsive drug delivery systems and molecular recognition phenomena. This work focuses on developing smart carriers that can respond to specific biological conditions.
"Host-Guest Interactions in Bioactive Supramolecular Systems"
Chemical Communications, 2024 • Under Review
Comprehensive study on host-guest interactions and their applications in developing smart materials for environmental and biological applications. The research explores binding selectivity and thermodynamic parameters governing molecular recognition.
"Advances in Stimuli-Responsive Supramolecular Materials"
Materials Chemistry Frontiers, 2023 • Published
Review article covering recent developments in stimuli-responsive materials based on supramolecular interactions. Discusses applications in sensors, actuators, and adaptive systems with focus on pH, temperature, and light-responsive materials.
"Conference Presentations & Collaborative Research"
Multiple International Conferences, 2022-2024
Engaged in interdisciplinary research collaborations focusing on the intersection of supramolecular chemistry with materials science and nanotechnology. Regular presenter at ACS National Meetings and International Symposiums on Supramolecular Chemistry.
