About Me

I am a dedicated researcher specializing in supramolecular chemistry, exploring the fascinating world of non-covalent interactions and molecular self-assembly. My work focuses on understanding how molecules interact through weak forces to create complex, functional structures with applications in materials science, drug delivery, and nanotechnology.

With a passion for innovation and discovery, I strive to contribute to the advancement of supramolecular science through rigorous research, collaboration, and the development of novel synthetic methodologies and analytical techniques.
